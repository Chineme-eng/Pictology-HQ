About Me / Business

Katherine (Chineme) Oduah is a Nigerian-American that came to the united states at 13 and began college at the age of 16. Her passion for Photography began when she was 7 years old, preferring to be behind the camera. Looking for a space to express her ideas and get inspiration from others, Katherine decided to create Pictology, Est 2025. Kathericreated Pictology with the hopes of making a community where artistic individuals are free to be themselves.
