The Manifesto

We believe the algorithm has commodified the human eye. We believe in the slow capture, the intentional frame, and the archival narrative.

Pictology HQ is a sanctuary for those who study the craft. We do not care for vanity metrics. We care for the light.

Real work. Real craft. No noise.
